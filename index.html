Governor Sindh IT Initiative

The Governor IT Initiative was launched in 2023 and has so far trained over 10,000 youth in Karachi. The program has been a great success, and Saylani Welfare Trust has played a vital role in its implementation.

Saylani was responsible for the development and implementation of the training curriculum, as well as the recruitment and training of the trainers. The Trust also provided the necessary infrastructure, including classrooms, computers, and internet access.

SMIT - Trained over 75,000 students IT skills

The Saylani Mass IT Training Program has been very successful in providing free IT training to young people in Pakistan. The program has trained over 75,000 students in various IT skills, and it has helped to create thousands of jobs.

The program's goal is to help Pakistan become a global leader in the IT industry. By providing free IT training to young people, the program is helping to build a skilled workforce that can contribute to the country's economic growth.
